Getting Started

Create a Note

Click the "New Note" button or press Ctrl+N to create a new note. Add a title and start typing your content.

Organize Notes

  • Pin: Keep important notes at the top of your list
  • Archive: Hide completed or old notes
  • Color Code: Use different colors to categorize notes
  • Search: Use the search box to find notes quickly

Tips & Tricks

  • Notes are automatically saved every 2 seconds
  • Use the color palette to visually organize your notes
  • Search works across note titles and content
  • Deleted notes can be restored from the Deleted tab
  • Check Analytics to see statistics about your notes
