Getting Started

Creating Notes

Click the "New Note" button or press Ctrl+N to create a new note. You can add a title, content, and choose a color.

Organizing Notes

Use colors to organize your notes by category or project. Pin important notes to keep them at the top. Archive old notes to keep your view clean.

Searching Notes

Use the search bar at the top to quickly find notes by title or content. Search is case-insensitive and updates in real-time.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use hashtags (#) in your notes to categorize them
  • Drag and drop notes to reorder them
  • Click a note to open it in the editor
  • Auto-save keeps your changes safe automatically
  • Access your notes from any device with your account
